## PhD and Honours Theses
You can find my PhD thesis [here](https://doi.org/10.26190/unsworks/24658). My PhD examined transport and mixing by *quasi-coherent objects* (eddies, fronts, gyres) in the ocean. I developed a range of numerical methods, algorithms and time-series analyses to solve oceanographic transport problems.

You can find my honours thesis [here](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1KQPP532bgxID28fgSMzsNTeJnl90xYIf/view?usp=sharing). My honours thesis examined the use of Backward Stochastic Difference Equations to derive pricing and hedging strategies for European, American and Game options in imperfect markets with frictions.
