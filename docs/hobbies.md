# Hobbies and Fun

Cycling is one of my favourite things. If I'm not at work, I'm probably out cycling.

In 2025, I "competed" in my first ultra-endurance cycling event, the **Utrecht Ultra**. I've ridden plenty of 100+ and 200+km rides, but never a 1000km ride in one go! I didn't quite get the nutrition right (eat more!) nor the sleep (weather forecasts were overly optimistic for the overnight temperatures).
